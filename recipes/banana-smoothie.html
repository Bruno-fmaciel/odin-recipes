Banana Smoothie

Description

You know that sunny day? Refresh with this delicious banana smoothie! It's simple and easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine banana, milk, vanilla, egg and sugar. Blend until smooth. Pour into a tall glass and top with a pinch of cinnamon.
